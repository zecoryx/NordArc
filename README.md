# ArcZen

Focus & Productivity - Block distracting sites with Commitment Lock.

ArcZen is a minimal browser extension designed to help you regain control over your digital life. Built on the philosophy of intentional browsing, it enforces strict boundaries to keep you focused on what truly matters.

## Core Features

- **Commitment Lock**: Block distracting websites with a strict enforcement policy.
- **Maintenance Policy**: Restrictions are only lifted for 10 minutes on the last day of every month (23:50 – 00:00). No shortcuts, no easy exits.
- **Shield**: Integrated ad-blocking technology to clean up your browsing experience.
- **Analytics**: Track your focus streak, time saved, and total distractions blocked.
- **Privacy First**: Local-first architecture ensures your browsing data stays on your device.

## Installation

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open your browser's extensions page (`chrome://extensions` or `edge://extensions`).
3. Enable **Developer mode**.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the `ArcZen` directory.

## Philosophy

ArcZen is not just a tool; it is a commitment. Unlike other blockers that are easy to bypass, ArcZen is built to respect your long-term goals over your short-term impulses.
